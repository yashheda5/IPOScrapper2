About CapitalNumbers Infotech

Established in 2012, CapitalNumbers Infotech Limited provides digital consulting and IT engineering services, offering software development solutions to businesses and startups globally. The company specializes in Digital Engineering, Data Analytics, AI/ML, Cloud Engineering, UI/UX Design, Blockchain, and AR/VR technologies. It operates on a project-based and Time & Material business model, serving over 250 active clients worldwide. The company holds ISO 9001, ISO 27001, and SOC 2 Type II certifications, which reflect its adherence to industry standards.

Strengths

  • Long-term customer relationships facilitate cross-selling opportunities.

Risks

  • Revenue is dependent on the USA and UK, any market changes could impact business.

  • Technological changes may cause obsolescence if unable to adapt.
  • Offer for Sale proceeds go to Promoter Selling Shareholders, not the company.
